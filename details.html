About IIT Bombay â€” quick history

IIT Bombay was established in 1958 with international collaboration and rapidly grew into a top engineering and research institute in India. The campus at Powai is an urban, research-driven hub with strong industry ties, a large entrepreneurship ecosystem (SINE), and major annual festivals (Techfest, Mood Indigo).

The institute emphasizes a mix of rigorous academics, research output, and entrepreneurship. It hosts multi-disciplinary research centers, postgraduate & doctoral programs, and strong industry engagement through placements, internships and sponsored projects.
